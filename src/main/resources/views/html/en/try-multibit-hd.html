Packed with useful features

MultiBit HD is a major upgrade to Classic and contains a huge number of useful features requested by Classic users. Here is a short summary:

  • no more worrying about losing private keys or passwords - use your "wallet words" (seed phrase) to restore them
  • simple and intuitive user interface - big accessible controls - great for touchscreens
  • Trezor and KeepKey support out of the box - just plug it in
  • fast, lightweight block chain download just like before
  • private Contacts database with notes and tags
  • private Payments database with integrated exchange rates
  • detailed integrated Help - no getting stuck
  • adjustable fees - a wide range to suit your needs
  • adjustable units - support for bits, ubits, millis etc
  • integrated exchange rates from many exchanges - practically every national currency is supported
  • export your transactions with historical spot rates to a financial package in standard CSV format - easier accounting
  • themes to suit different lighting conditions
  • better language coverage than Classic

MultiBit HD is open source but is not free

Each spend incurs an extra 1,000 satoshi fee (about 0.5 US cents) that is bundled up and sent to the MultiBit development team every 15 to 25 sends.

You can find out more by following the link.

MultiBit HD and Classic run together happily on the same machine

MultiBit HD has been designed to be completely separate from Classic. They can both safely operate on the same machine without interfering with each other.

Why not try MultiBit HD and explore its new features? When you want to make MultiBit HD your primary wallet simply spend your MultiBit Classic balance into MultiBit HD.

Download MultiBit HD

You can download MultiBit HD from the front page. Read and accept the terms and conditions, then click the button for your operating system. After a moment your download will begin and you will be shown installation instructions including a YouTube video on how to set up your wallet.
