Change PIN

When you have a Trezor or KeepKey attached, from the Tools screen select "Change PIN‚Äù.

The Trezor / KeepKey device will show a PIN matrix on its screen. To enter a new PIN you must look at where the digits of your PIN are on the Trezor / KeepKey screen and then click the corresponding button on the MultiBit HD screen. This "digit position" technique prevents electronic eavesdroppers from discovering your PIN.

You can repeat digits in your PIN and you can make it up to 10 digits long.

  1. Enter your current PIN to unlock the wallet. Click Next
  2. Enter the replacement PIN. Click Next
  3. Re-enter the replacement PIN to avoid typos. Click Next
  4. You are then shown a report screen showing outcome of the operation.
