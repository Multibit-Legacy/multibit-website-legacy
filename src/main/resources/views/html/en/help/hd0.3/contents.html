MultiBit HD Version 0.2.x help

MultiBit HD has been designed to be as easy to use as possible but Bitcoin itself can take some getting used to. These articles aim to provide useful explanations of common issues that people encounter.

Please take the time to read our Bitcoin FAQ which will give you a useful working knowledge of Bitcoin and may even inspire you to start your own Bitcoin business.
