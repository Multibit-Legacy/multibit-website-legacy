Why choose MultiBit HD ?

MultiBit HD is:

  • Quick and easy to install across all major desktop operating systems (Windows, OS X, Linux)
  • Secure by design - all your data is kept encrypted on your local machine
  • Faster to synchronize - usually a minute or so
  • Deterministic - you have a seed phrase which you can use to reclaim your bitcoin
  • Decentralised - no fixed servers only the Bitcoin network
  • International - multiple languages (35+ at the last release)

If you are an ordinary user of Bitcoin and simply want a wallet that just works without hassle, then MultiBit HD is the right choice for you.
